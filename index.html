Work Experience and Acknowledgments

MaY 2018 - Present

AtmenUhr
Innovation Project

The AtmenUhr project developed by the Idee group, whose purpose is to give technological assistance to smokers in their process of quitting tobacco, this process will be supported by a smart spray that let the user knows when and how to take a dose of nicotine orally, the time and quantity (the regulation of use) are given by an initial parameterization with basic data about the user addiction problem. The device is connected to a mobile application and all the information retrieved from the sensors in the spray will be processed to give the user useful information and real time advance about the quitting process. The device can also offer medical monitoring to the user for a stricter process with the validity of professionals in the health area, these professionals will answer any questions the user could have and will give a more personalized list of advices. The complete system can be managed by the user in a common language virtual assistant that gives ease of handling and interaction. The nature of the system of a strong interconnected network gives the possiblity in the future to tolerate inter-user communication as a social network for sharing experiences to help each other to progress in the common goal.
